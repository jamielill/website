Jamie Lillington

Junior 3D Environment Artist

Hey! I'm Jamie, a 21-year-old Games Design and Development student at the University of Greenwich. I enjoy participating in gamejams, Dev meet up events and of course gaming with my friends. The pressure of having to create a game in a 36-48hr time frame really motivates me to be my most creative. As well as this, in my spare time I love trying to recreate things I see around me in 3D! This could be a friend's artwork, a silly GIF or even my Bedroom. Previously I studied A-levels in Computer Science, Mathematics and Psychology.

Hope you enjoy my portfolio!

Gamejam Unity

MoleSweeper

This was my first ever gameJam with the theme of 'Roots', I worked with 3 of my coursemates and we created a two player tile based top down game inspired by 'mine sweeper'.

Gamejam Unity

OrganSweeper

For my second gameJam, I worked with the same team as the first, this theme was 'Welfare'. OrganSweeper is a fast-paced 2D hospital game made in Unity where the player has 12 patients to look after.

Gamejam Unity

MallSweeper

MallSweeper is a 2D puzzle game set in a mall, developed in unity. The Player must find dead bodies in stores around the mall and harvest there organs to summon a mysterious creature.
