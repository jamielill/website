About Me:

Hey there! I'm Jamie Lillington, a 19-year-old student currently pursuing a degree in Games Design and Development at the University of Greenwich. My passion lies in collaborative game development, and I thrive in the fast-paced environment of game jams, creating a whole game from a theme in only a short amount of time and working with my friends. Additionally, I enjoy using Blender to recreate real-world environments into 3D models. Previously I studied A-levels in Computer Science, Mathematics and Psychology.

Gamejams

Gamejam Unity

MoleSweeper

This was my first ever gameJam with the theme of 'Roots', I worked with 3 of my coursemates and we created a two player tile based top down game inspired by 'mine sweeper'. The aim is to dig up a mole before your opponent does. However you can also dig up different root vegetables such as the hot potato which explodes a 3x3 grid of tiles, and the beatroot which punches the player to the edge of the map.

Gamejam Unity

OrganSweeper

For my second gameJam, I worked with the same team as the first, this theme was 'Welfare'. OrganSweeper is a fast-paced 2D hospital game made in Unity where the player has 12 patients to look after. All of them have problems with their organs, your task is to remove the bad ones, throw them in the bin and replace them with the good organs of fellow patients. Not everyone can be saved!

Gamejam Unity

No one can hear you laugh in space

The theme for this gameJam was "make me laugh". We decided to make a two player 2D puzzle game set on a space station. One player has a freeze ray that can freeze objects and doors, the other has an anti-gravity gun which can move objects. The idea is that the combination of these weapons will allow the players to mess with each other and create funny situations.

Gamejam Unity

Confectionery Confusion

The theme of this gameJam was "moderation". I worked in a much large group of 9 people for this jam. We created a puzzle game set in a sweet shop, NPC's ask for a weight of sweets but your scale is broken! So you must guess (moderate) the amount of sweets to give each person.

University

Arduino nuclear reactor panel game

This group project is a bit different. Our task was to create a custom controller as well as a game in unity to go along with it. Our group decided to make a nuclear reactor control panel puzzle game similar to "please don't touch anything" and "Keep talking and nobody explodes" I designed and created the physical controller.

Unity

Anti-Sailors

This project is a basic top down roguelike game incorporating OOP principles, set in the future where sailing is outlawed and the Sea is patroled by pirates and high tech police boats. Your goal is to defeat them all.

Unreal

bartender blitz

This is a solo project with all 3D assets created by me. Your role is a bartender that must serve drinks to life sized glasses of alcohol before they get 'pissed' and attack you. If this happens you must make shots and cocktails such as the Jager Bomb which will explode the enemies.

Games Development Society

As part of my Second year at Uni, I helped restart the Games Development Society along with 5 of my friends, with my role being the treasurer.

Retro Games Night

Our first event was retro games night. We connected old consoles such as: Wii, MegaDrive, Playstation 2 and NES to the screens in our student bar. The main attraction was sing star (a karaoke game) on the PS2.

"Fight" Night

For this event we hosted a Street fighter 6 Tournament and I made the custom medal for the winner! As well as this, we had Mortal Kombat X, Tekken 8 and Smash Bros. Brawl for people who wanted a more casual experience.

Quiz Night

we had a 60 question video game quiz with free pizza! We had three rounds: easy, medium and hard. With questions including 'what game is this image from' and historic gaming events.
