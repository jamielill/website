My Uni Room - Rendered in cycles

WIP - I'm recreating my room in blender (since March 2024) with as much detail as possible, including measuring most things within a 1 cm accuracy. I first measured the floor print of the entire room and then started to block out my desk area. As can be seen in the first photo below which was Rendered with Eevee. I then added some objects, such as a Rubik's cube, a book and monitor. By the end of the project, I aim to create every object that is seen in the photo above.
