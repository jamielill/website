my uni room - Rendered in cycles

I recreated my room in blender (March 2024) with as much detail as possible, including measuring most things within a 1 cm accuracy. I first measured the floor print of the entire room and then started to block out my desk area. As can be seen in the first photo below which was Rendered with Eevee. I then added some objects, such as a Rubik's cube and a book.
