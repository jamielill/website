Spray Cap

I have been interested in graffiti for a long time so I decided to model a spray paint nozzle/cap in June 2022. The curves were created by using a 'difference' boolean modifier with a cyclinder as the mask mesh.

Specs:
>>> Software: Blender
>>> Final Render: Cycles
>>> Tris: 1.75k
