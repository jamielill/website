Spray Cap

I have been interested in graffiti for a long time so I decided to model a spray paint nozzle/cap
>>> V1.0 in June 2022, the curves were created by using a 'difference' boolean modifier with a cyclinder as the mask mesh.
>>> V1.1 in August 2024, the cap material was made more shiny and a background was added.

Specs:
>>> Software: Blender
>>> Final Render: Cycles
>>> Tris: 1.75k

Potential improvements:
>>> Better matching of material colours
>>> Optimise tri count
