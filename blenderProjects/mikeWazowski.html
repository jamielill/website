Mike Wazowski - Rendered in Eevee

I created this model based on a friend's drawing of Mike Wazowski from Pixar's Monsters Inc. The initial model was made in May 2023 in around 2 hours, after the friend sent the sketch pictured below into our uni group chat and I wondered how easy it would be to remake in Blender.

After gaining more experience with Blender I recently (March 2024) improved apon the model. To remove the Harsh edges I used the subsurface division modifier as well as shade auto-smooth. In the future I plan to rig the model to allow for the mouth to open and close
