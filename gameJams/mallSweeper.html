MallSweeper, a gamejam project

MallSweeper is a 2D puzzle game set in a mall, developed in unity. The Player must find dead bodies in stores around the mall and harvest there organs to summon a mysterious creature. This is my third gameJam working with the EJAM Entertainment team. The theme for the game jam was "what lies in store"

Play the game here!

My contribution

My main role was 3D modeller, creating the 3D environment and mole character. I also helped with the level design and organ power-ups mechanic.
