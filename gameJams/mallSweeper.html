MallSweeper | Project Type: Game Jam | Theme: What lies in store

MallSweeper is a 2D puzzle game set in a mall, developed in unity. The Player must find dead bodies in stores around the mall and harvest there organs to summon a mysterious creature. This is my third gameJam working with the EJAM Entertainment team.

My contribution

My main role was 3D modeller, creating the 3D environment and mole character. I also helped with the level design and organ power-ups mechanic.

Software used

Unity, Adobe Photoshop, Blender
