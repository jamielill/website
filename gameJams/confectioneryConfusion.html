Confectionery Confusion | Project Type: Game Jam | Theme: Moderation

Info

I worked in a much large group of 9 people for this jam. We created a puzzle game set in a sweet shop, NPC's ask for a weight of sweets but your scale is broken! So you must guess (moderate) the amount of sweets to give each person.

My contribution

My role was content intergrator, ensuring that all visual assets were imported correctly without compression. I also created the UI menu Assets and Main Menu.

Unity, Adobe Photoshop
