Bubble Man Chomp, a gamejam project

The theme of this gameJam was "bubble". I worked in a group of 5 and we created a wacky bubblegum chewing and blowing simulator. The player must press keyboard or controller keys in the right order in Dance Dance Revolution Style to chew the bubblegum and then hold spacebar to blow the bubble!

Play the game here!

My contribution

My role was 3D Environment and Character Artist. I created the "Bubble Man's" head model, as well as the background candy shop.
