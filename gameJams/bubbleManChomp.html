Bubble Man Chomp | Project Type: Game Jam | Theme: Bubble

Info

This project was created as part of the Global Game Jam 2025. I worked in a group of 5 and we created a wacky bubblegum chewing and blowing simulator. The player must press keyboard or controller keys in the right order in Dance Dance Revolution Style to chew the bubblegum and then hold spacebar to blow the bubble!

My contribution

My role was 3D Environment and Character Artist. I created the "Bubble Man's" head model, as well as the background candy shop.

Software used

Unity, Adobe Photoshop, Blender
