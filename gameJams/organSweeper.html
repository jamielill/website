OrganSweeper | Project Type: Game Jam | Theme: Welfare

Info

For my second game jam, I worked with the same team as the first. OrganSweeper is a fast-paced 2D hospital game made in Unity where the player has 12 patients to look after. All of them have problems with their organs, your task is to remove the bad ones, throw them in the bin and replace them with the good organs of fellow patients. Not everyone can be saved!

My contribution

My main role was content integrator ensuring visual assets were imported without compression. I also programmed the human organ storage system.

Software used

Unity, Adobe Photoshop
